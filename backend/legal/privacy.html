1. Overview

GirlMath ("the App") is published by Code-WRX. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide and improve the service:

  • Device Identifier: A random anonymous ID generated on your device, used solely for daily rate limiting of price checks. This ID is not linked to your Apple ID or any personal information.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase data is managed entirely by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment information.
  • Product Searches: When you use the price check feature, the product name you enter is sent to our server to search for prices. We do not permanently store your search queries.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Contacts, photos, or any other personal files
  • Browsing history or activity outside the App
  • Any data from other apps on your device

4. Data Storage

All app data (your settings, justification history, bill reminders) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For managing subscriptions and in-app purchases. See their privacy policy.
  • SerpAPI: For fetching product prices from public shopping results. No personal data is sent to SerpAPI.
  • Apple App Store: For processing payments. See Apple's privacy policy.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data on our servers. Rate-limiting data (anonymous device IDs) is stored in server memory only and is cleared automatically every 24 hours or when the server restarts.

8. Your Rights

Since we store virtually no personal data, there is little to request. However, you may:

  • Delete all local app data by uninstalling the App
  • Manage or cancel your subscription through Apple Settings
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
